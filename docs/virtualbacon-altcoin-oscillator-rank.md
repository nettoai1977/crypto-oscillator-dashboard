# VirtualBacon — Altcoin Oscillator Rank (April 10, 2026)

## Core Thesis
Most altcoins trend DOWN vs Bitcoin over time. The ones worth buying are "oscillators" — coins that trade sideways with strong bounces, not coins that keep bleeding against BTC.

Ignore dollar prices. **The ALT/BTC ratio is the truth.**

## Two Filters

### Filter 1: "This vs Last Cycle Top"
Compares this cycle's ALT/BTC peak to last cycle's peak.
- **Positive** = outperformed BTC cycle-over-cycle
- **Negative** = weaker bounce each cycle
- **Below -65%** = the oscillator is dying

### Filter 2: "% Above 5yr Low"
Current ALT/BTC ratio vs the coin's worst-ever level.
- **Above 25%** = healthy distance from floor
- **Below 25%** = sitting near all-time worst vs Bitcoin

## Tier System

| Tier | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **S** | Outperformed BTC (positive vs last cycle + above floor) |
| **A** | Strong oscillators (within -35% + above 25% of floor) |
| **B** | Weaker oscillators (-35% to -65% + above floor) |
| **F** | Failed: oscillator broken, avoid for next cycle |

## Key Takeaways (VirtualBacon)

1. **67% of veteran altcoins failed the oscillator test.** Their BTC ratio bounces are getting weaker each cycle, or they are sitting at all-time lows. These are permanent avoid.
2. **Only 7 out of 70 veterans (10%) outperformed Bitcoin this cycle:** CFX, FET, POL, ZEC, RENDER, TRX, XRP. That is the entire S Tier.
3. **10 coins are strong oscillators (A Tier):** SOL, ETH, BNB, XMR, XRP, FLOKI. They lose ground each cycle but still bounce hard enough to trade.
4. **The two filters that matter:** "vs last cycle" shows if the oscillator is dying. "% above 5yr low" shows if there is any floor left. Both need to pass.
5. **Build your next-cycle watchlist from S and A Tier.** Buy when ALT/BTC ratios hit historical support in the next bear. Ignore dollar prices; the ratio is the truth.

## Overall Assessment

### What's Strong
1. **Data-driven, not narrative-driven** — 95 altcoins, 5-year ratios, two clear filters. No vibes.
2. **Core insight is correct** — ALT/BTC ratios decay for most coins. 78% permanently broken.
3. **Oscillator concept is real** — SOL, ETH, BNB bounce enough to trade despite losing ground.
4. **Surprise entries are the edge** — INJ (+130%), CFX (+125%) outperforming BTC while in F-tier.

### What's Weak
1. **Small sample size** — 4 S-tier, 6 A-tier. Could be noise.
2. **Cycle timing is imprecise** — "buy at support" but no exact entry definition.
3. **Assumes cycles repeat** — ETFs changed Bitcoin's market structure. Institutional money ≠ retail cycles.
4. **No risk framework** — Tells you WHAT but not HOW MUCH or WHEN TO SELL.
5. **Single-cycle coins underdeveloped** — TAO, SUI, ZRO need a framework.

### Verdict
**It's a filter, not a strategy.**

Excellent at eliminating garbage (78% avoid). But you still need:
- Entry timing (ALT/BTC support definition)
- Risk management (position sizing per coin)
- Exit strategy (profit taking rules)
- A signal scanner (catch bounces automatically)

**Best use:** Filter first (S/A tier only), then apply your own timing and risk on top.
