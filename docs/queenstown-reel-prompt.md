# Queenstown Tourism Reel — Director's Cut Prompt

**Created:** 2026-06-24
**Format:** Instagram Reel, vertical 9:16, 10 seconds
**Location:** Queenstown, New Zealand only (no Milford, no Glenorchy, no Arrowtown)

---

## Prompt

**[Attach: reference photo of Michael]**

Create a cinematic 10-second tourism reel for Instagram, vertical 9:16 format. Use the reference image as the main subject — maintain his face and appearance throughout. All scenes are set in Queenstown, New Zealand. No other locations.

**Subject:** Michael, aged 48, Indian origin (Kerala), living in Christchurch, New Zealand. He wears a cap or hat suited to each scene and glasses/spectacles throughout. His look is consistent — same person, same visual signature, different moments. He is a real person exploring Queenstown, not a model performing for camera.

**Color Bible:** Dominant palette is deep blues, teals, and turquoise — the glacial blue of Lake Wakatipu, the alpine blue of the Remarkables, the crisp high-altitude sky. Contrast with warm golden-hour highlights on skin and foreground elements. Cool shadows, warm highlights — the "blockbuster look." No warm filters. The light should feel clean, sharp, distinctly Queenstown.

**Emotional Arc:** Hook (0-2s) → Build (2-7s) → Payoff (7-10s)

**Scene 1 (0–1.5s) — HOOK:**
Extreme wide drone shot, dawn. Michael stands alone on the Queenstown Pier, arms slightly out, breathing in the cold morning air. He wears a dark beanie and glasses. Lake Wakatipu is mirror-still, reflecting the Remarkables in deep glacial blue. Single figure against vast landscape. Camera slowly pushes in. No words needed — this image is the reason you came.

**Scene 2 (1.5–3s) — LIFT:**
Michael riding the Skyline Gondola up Bob's Peak. Camera faces him through the glass cabin window. Behind him, Queenstown shrinks below — lake, town, mountains unfolding in layers of blue. He wears a clean baseball cap and glasses, one hand on the glass, looking out with quiet awe. The light shifts from town warmth to alpine cool as he ascends.

**Scene 3 (3–4s) — THRILL:**
Michael on the Skyline Luge, racing downhill. Baseball cap backward, glasses on, leaning into a turn with a wide grin. Camera tracks alongside at speed. Pine trees blur on the edges. The Remarkables sit in the background, cold and blue against the warm luge track. Pure fun.

**Scene 4 (4–5s) — SPEED:**
Michael on the Shotover Jet, camera mounted low on the boat. He wears a baseball cap secured with a strap, glasses catching spray. The jet carves through the narrow Shotover Canyon, turquoise water spraying in slow-motion droplets. Michael is laughing, gripping the handle. Backlit spray catching golden light. The canyon walls are raw grey rock.

**Scene 5 (5–6s) — NATURE:**
Tracking shot — Michael hiking the Queenstown Hill walk. He wears a wide-brim outdoor hat and glasses, backpack on. The trail winds through native bush, then opens to the panoramic viewpoint. Lake Wakatipu stretches below in deep blue. The Remarkables fill the horizon. Wind lifts the brim of his hat. Camera follows from slightly behind — he's alone up here, and that's the point.

**Scene 6 (6–7s) — INDULGENCE:**
Close-up — Michael biting into a Fergburger, cap slightly tilted, juice running down his hand. Background is the bustling Queenstown Mall, slightly out of focus. Warm tungsten light from the shop sign. His glasses reflect the glowing "FERGBURGER" sign. A moment of pure, unhurried satisfaction. This is not food content — this is a ritual.

**Scene 7 (7–8.5s) — STILLNESS:**
Michael sitting on a bench in the Queenstown Gardens, lake visible through the trees. He wears a relaxed cap and glasses, coffee in hand. Autumn leaves drift past. The light is soft, diffused through branches. A quiet moment between adventures. Camera holds still — no movement, just breathing room.

**Scene 8 (8.5–10s) — PAYOFF:**
Golden hour. Michael sits on the Queenstown waterfront, legs dangling off the edge of the pier, cap on, glasses reflecting the sunset. He's looking out at the Remarkables turning pink-gold over the still lake. Camera slowly pulls back to reveal the full panorama — mountains, lake, sky, one person. A moment of stillness after all the energy. This is the frame people screenshot and share. This is the Queenstown that stays with you.

**Technical Notes:**
- Michael's hat/cap changes per scene but his glasses remain consistent throughout
- Smooth transitions between scenes — use motion blur or whip-pan bridges
- Maintain consistent lighting progression: dawn cool → midday energy → golden hour warmth
- Keep Michael's outfit consistent within each scene
- Subtle lens flares acceptable on golden-hour shots
- No text overlays, no filters — clean cinematic grade only
- All locations are within Queenstown — no Milford Sound, no Glenorchy, no Arrowtown
