# Auckland Time-Freeze Reel — "Stop the City"

**Created:** 2026-06-25
**Format:** Instagram Reel, vertical 9:16, 2 × 10-second clips
**Location:** Auckland, New Zealand
**Concept:** Michael snaps his fingers and freezes time across Auckland. Two clips, two frozen zones, same mechanic.

---

## WHY TWO CLIPS

The time-freeze concept has enough visual texture for two self-contained 10s reels:

- **Clip 1: "Stop the City"** — Urban core freeze (Queen Street + Sky Tower). Dense, chaotic, neon energy.
- **Clip 2: "Stop the Harbour"** — Waterfront freeze (Viaduct + Ferry Building). Open, airy, golden hour calm.

Same snap. Different Auckland. Together they form a "day in Auckland" arc — from street chaos to harbour stillness.

**Why this works for our playbook:**
- Each clip is a standalone hook (can be posted 2-3 days apart)
- "Part 2" creates return viewers ("I saw Part 1, what's next?")
- Doubles content output from one concept
- Each clip has its own emotional tone (energy → calm) so they don't feel repetitive

## CLIP 1 — "STOP THE CITY" (10s)

### Emotional Arc
Hook (0-2s) → Escalation (2-5s) → Play (5-8s) → Payoff (8-10s)

### Timeline

**0–1.5s — HOOK:**
Extreme close-up. Michael's face fills the frame. Intense stare directly into camera. Queen Street neon blurred behind him. He raises his right hand and SNAPS. Golden shockwave bursts from fingertips — air distorts, light refracts.
> Sound: deafening city crowd → SNAP → deep bass drop → absolute silence

**1.5–3s — REVEAL:**
Wide shot, camera pulls back fast. Queen Street frozen mid-chaos — a food vendor mid-pour, pedestrians mid-stride, a busker's coin suspended in air, neon signs reflecting off wet pavement. Michael walks forward, calm, unbothered.
> Sound: silence. Wind only.

**3–5s — SKY TOWER:**
Tracking shot. Michael walks toward camera, Sky Tower looming behind him, frozen fountain in the foreground. He glances up at the tower, smirks, keeps walking. Volumetric neon light catches suspended rain droplets.
> Sound: echoing footsteps on wet concrete

**5–7s — PLAY:**
Michael ducks under a frozen street vendor's steam cloud, reaches out and pretends to grab a floating dumpling from a suspended display. Camera whip-pans to follow him. He tosses the imaginary dumpling into his mouth, chews, nods approvingly.
> Sound: exaggerated crunch (the only sound)

**7–9s — PAYOFF:**
Medium close-up. Michael faces camera. Confident smirk. He raises his hand again — freezes for a beat — SNAPS. Reverse shockwave blasts outward. Everything resumes — the vendor pours, pedestrians walk, the busker catches his coin. Camera pushes through the crowd.
> Sound: SNAP → reverse bass drop → deafening city roar returns

**9–10s — WIDE REVEAL:**
Quick drone shot pulling up from Queen Street, revealing the full Auckland skyline — Sky Tower, Harbour Bridge, Waitematā Harbour. Golden hour glow on buildings.
> Sound: city ambience fades to quiet

## CLIP 2 — "STOP THE HARBOUR" (10s)

### Emotional Arc
Callback hook (0-2s) → Wonder (2-5s) → Stillness (5-8s) → Payoff (8-10s)

### Timeline

**0–1.5s — HOOK (CALLBACK):**
Wide shot — Viaduct Harbour, golden hour. Seagulls frozen mid-flight, a waiter frozen mid-pour, sailboats suspended. Michael walks into frame from the right, hand already raised. He SNAPS.
> Sound: harbour ambience → SNAP → silence

**1.5–3.5s — FROZEN HARBOUR:**
Tracking shot. Michael walks along the Viaduct promenade. The waiter's wine pour is a frozen golden arc. A cyclist is mid-pedal. Seagulls hang in the sky like ornaments. The Harbour Bridge stretches behind, cars frozen on it. Golden light on everything.
> Sound: silence. Distant water lapping.

**3.5–5.5s — FERRY BUILDING:**
Michael reaches the Auckland Ferry Building. Clock tower frozen, tourists frozen mid-photo. He leans against the railing, looks out at the harbour. A frozen ice cream drips — he reaches over and catches the suspended drop on his finger. Smirks.
> Sound: single water droplet echo

**5.5–7.5s — STILLNESS:**
Close-up — Michael sitting on the waterfront edge, legs dangling over the water. He looks out at the frozen harbour — sailboats, seagulls, reflections all suspended. He takes a deep breath, closes his eyes for a beat. The world is still. This is the calm.
> Sound: silence. Just breathing.

**7.5–9s — SNAP:**
Michael opens his eyes. Faces camera. Confident smirk. He snaps. Reverse shockwave — the waiter finishes pouring, seagulls fly, sailboat sails fill with wind, the cyclist pedals away. The harbour comes alive.
> Sound: SNAP → reverse bass → harbour sounds return (seagulls, water, chatter)

**9–10s — WIDE REVEAL:**
Drone pulls back — full Viaduct Harbour panorama, Auckland skyline glowing golden, Harbour Bridge, Rangitoto Island in the distance. Michael is a small figure on the promenade, looking out.
> Sound: golden hour ambience, fades
